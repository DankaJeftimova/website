Personal Interests & Hobbies

Outside of academics and competitions, I enjoy creative and reflective activities that help me relax, focus, and develop patience.

Reading

Reading is my primary way to unwind. I enjoy a wide range of genres, with a particular interest in classic literature and science fiction. Reading helps me improve my vocabulary, concentration, and ability to process complex narratives and ideas, which is valuable for both creative writing and problem-solving.

Embroidery

This hands-on hobby requires detailed focus and patience. I enjoy planning out complex patterns and seeing them come to life through careful, repetitive stitching. It's a satisfying contrast to the digital world of programming.

Crochet

Crochet is a great exercise in pattern recognition. I enjoy the process of quickly creating textured items, from small amigurumi figures to larger functional pieces, applying basic, repeatable logic to produce varied results.
